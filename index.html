What is MicroByte?

MicroByte is a friendly AI chatbot that learns from interactions. When unsure about a question, it kindly asks for guidance. Through user input, it keeps growing and providing better responses over time. Your engagement helps shape MicroByte into a more knowledgeable and helpful companion.

What makes this special?

With MicroByte, you have the ability to have a simple fast AI on your device even offline. As this does not use GPT, Microbyte ensures privacy and fastens reply speed.

What you can do with This?

You can have answers for simple questions like What is the capital of india? or What is the boiling point of alcohol?
